AJCP reference: age-predicted cellularity = 65 − (0.3 × age). Acceptance band per age < 20 years: ±20 percentage points; age ≥ 20 years: ±15 percentage points (Vasef et al., AJCP 2026, see source link above). Classic rule of thumb: normal cellularity ≈ 100 − age. Both are population-level estimates, not diagnostic thresholds — interpret alongside clinical context, comorbidities, and marrow patchiness.
